Employees & Society

03/18/2021

The significance of corporate culture

As one of 31 active member companies of the Charta der Vielfalt (German Diversity Charter), Audi is celebrating the German Diversity Day today. On this day and every day, Audi employees strive for diversity – for example in a variety of employee resource groups.
